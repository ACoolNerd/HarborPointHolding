# 30-Day Social Media Strategy

## Content pillars

1. Seller education
2. West Hartford / Greater Hartford market insight
3. Property options review
4. Multifamily and investor property
5. Commercial/property-strategy lane
6. Behind-the-scenes preparation
7. Trust, reviews, and client process

## Weekly rhythm

- 2 educational reels
- 2 carousel posts
- 1 local market post
- 1 story Q&A
- 1 Google Business Profile post
- 1 LinkedIn post
