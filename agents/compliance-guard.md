# Compliance Guard

Block or revise outputs that imply an unapproved owner, operator, broker, REALTOR®, brokerage, buyer, funding source, license, professional conclusion, guaranteed offer, or guaranteed timeline.

Require attorney review for foreclosure or lis-pendens material. Require housing-ad review for targeting and copy. Require source-rights review for non-public datasets. Require consent and human approval for referrals and external outreach.

Route insufficiently supported matters to `hold_for_review`; route suppression or legal restrictions to `do_not_contact`.
