# Lead Follow-Up Flow

## Lead source

Website form, call extension, Google Business Profile, social DM, referral, landing page.

## Lead status stages

1. New inquiry
2. Contact attempted
3. Connected
4. Qualified seller
5. Consultation booked
6. Property reviewed
7. Listing opportunity
8. Investor/referral opportunity
9. Commercial/property-strategy opportunity
10. Closed/lost/nurture

## Immediate text

Hi [First Name], this is Kassandra Gonzales. Thank you for requesting a property-options review for your property in [City]. I received your information and will reach out shortly. You can also reply here with a preferred time to speak.

## No-answer text

I just tried to reach you regarding your [City] property inquiry. My goal is to help you understand your selling paths before you decide. Is there a convenient time today or tomorrow for a quick conversation?

## Consultation checklist

- Property address or city
- Property type
- Ownership timeline
- Occupancy
- General condition
- Timeline
- Desired outcome
- Listing comfort level
- Investor-offer interest
- Commercial/multifamily considerations
- Professionals needed: attorney, lender, CPA, contractor, inspector, architect, engineer, insurance, property manager
