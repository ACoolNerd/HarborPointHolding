Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 17, 2026

Harbor Point Holding ("we," "us") respects your privacy. This policy explains what we collect through harborpointholding.com and how we use it.

Information We Collect

  • Information you provide: when you complete an intake on this site, we collect your name, email address, phone number, the town and type of property involved, and your stated goals and timeline.
  • Automatic information: standard web logs and, where enabled, analytics data such as pages visited and referral source.

How We Use It

  • To respond to your inquiry, prepare offers or strategies you requested, and schedule consultations you book.
  • To follow up on your inquiry by email, phone, or text. You can opt out of follow-up at any time by replying STOP to texts, using the unsubscribe link in emails, or contacting us directly.
  • To understand which of our pages and campaigns are useful, using aggregate source data.

How We Store and Share It

  • Inquiry data is stored in our customer relationship management system (HubSpot) and scheduling system (Calendly), each governed by their own security practices and privacy policies.
  • We do not sell your personal information.
  • We share information only with service providers that operate this site and our communications, or when required by law, or with professionals (such as attorneys or title companies) engaged in a transaction you pursue with us — with your knowledge.

Your Choices

  • You may request access to, correction of, or deletion of your information at any time by emailing us.
  • Connecticut residents may have additional rights under the Connecticut Data Privacy Act, including the rights to access, correct, delete, and obtain a copy of personal data. To exercise these rights, contact us using the details below.
